VOL Health™ Volatility Index

Directional diagnostic that scores the Trinity of Volatility, Operations & Labor across ten drivers — census, OT, PBJ exposure, survey risk, reimbursement, reputation, leadership bandwidth, and more. Designed for a single building or an entire portfolio.

  • Why VOL Health: Make endemic volatility visible in one stability score and economic range.
  • How it works: Ten inputs roll into a 0–100 stability score and directional annual economics.
  • Details: Built for senior living operators; calibrated for PBJ, OT, survey risk, and portfolio views.
Launch Volatility Index

Workforce Stability Index (WSI)

Companion view focused on the strength of your workforce pipeline. Scores staffing volatility, financial exposure, operational & compliance risk, and workforce pipeline health into a single WSI score.

  • Why WSI: Quantify how unstable schedules, vacancies, and turnover drag operations and margin.
  • How it works: Mix of direct inputs or portfolio distributions roll up into a 0–100 WSI.
  • Details: Shows volatility cost, stabilizable upside, and workforce drag as dollar ranges.
